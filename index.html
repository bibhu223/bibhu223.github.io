Biography

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher in the Department of Robotics and Mechatronics Engineering at the Daegu Gyeongbuk Institute of Science and Technology (DGIST), Republic of Korea. I work in the Autonomy and Perceptual Robotics Lab under the supervision of Prof. Giseop Kim.

My research lies at the intersection of magnetic sensing, mobile robotics, simultaneous localization and mapping (SLAM), sensor fusion, and artificial intelligence. I develop magnetic sensing and multimodal perception methods for robust robot localization and navigation in GPS-denied and perceptually challenging environments.

I received my Ph.D. from the Indian Institute of Technology Hyderabad, where my research focused on magnetic thin films, exchange-biased structures, and high-sensitivity magnetic sensors.
